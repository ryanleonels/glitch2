Numbers Get Very Big - Version 1.2

About

This number increaser made by the Youtuber Incremental Gameplays.
The number starts at 5e-324 and slowly increases every second.
Since the increase is so slow, there has been a skip 1 min-hour button.
To get past 1.79e308, a certain number variable was used.

The use of curly brackets and numbers comes after 1e1000000.
The number in the curly bracket represents the number of e's there is.
Therefore, it saves tons of space and time to look at and maintain.

The inspiration of this comes from Douglas Shamlin Jr, but it was not as
complete as this one will be in the future. Expecting more awesome updates
soon!

Anyways please take a moment and see the numbers rise up quickly!
You will enjoy it if you are a big number fan. Can be boring to watch.
