Help Tab

How to play this game? You have come to the right place.
In this game, magnets and gold magnets are the main currency of the game.
Each button click gives you a certain number of magnets.
There is 4 different buttons for continuously increasing your magnet multiplier, if you cannot afford the larger ones.
At 500 magnets, you can get Gold Magnets and increase your magnet multiplier by 1.3x.
Buying Gold Magnets means all other magnets are removed.
