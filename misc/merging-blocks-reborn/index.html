How to play Merge Blocks v3.1

When you first start playing, the first thing you wanna do is press the "Merge" button.

When you do press the "Merge" button, there is a cooldown on how long before you can merge again.

When you do press the "Merge" button, you can increase the tier of your blocks to get more money.

Once you get 100 money you can buy Faster Blocks to further decrease the cooldown before you need to merge again.

Once you get 400 money you can buy Better Blocks to increase the spawning block's tier.

Once you get 2800 money you can buy Doubled Money to increase the chance when a block spawns, you get 2x more money.

The game might get a bit boring after that, so you always got a merge counter with you.

But once you get over 200,000 money you have the ability to prestige for Super Money and higher boost.

Once you get enough Super Money you can buy 2 upgrades.

Starting at 7 Super Money is more Faster Blocks upgrades.

This will be even more helpful for speeding up your progression.

Starting at 35 Super Money is more Doubled Money upgrades.

This will also be even more helpful for speeding up your progression.

There might be much more than this after that. The game is only in construction. In the future you might see more mechanics.

This game currently has a lot of blocks/barrels so have a good journey reaching the last one.

Make sure to export your save once in a little while just in case you lose your progress.

This game has some bugs, like no moving your fingers to merge at all, and blocks unlocked bug, not showing latest tier.

Hopefully some of the bugs will get fixed very soon.

Enjoy your gameplay!
